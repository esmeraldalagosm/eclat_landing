The concept

The hidden rabbit awaits you

Inspired by the French tradition of gift-giving and the Chinese blind box culture, ÉCLAT merges the thrill of discovery with the craftsmanship of luxury confectionery. Six richly flavoured chocolate eggs nestle inside a hand-finished walnut box lined in deep velvet.

Gather around. Choose. Break open. One egg holds a hand-painted Limoges porcelain rabbit charm — a symbol of fortune, renewal, and the joy of being chosen.

The ritual

How to play Éclat

I
Gather

Bring family or friends around the table. Open the walnut box together and reveal the six wrapped eggs in their velvet nest.

II
Choose

Each person selects one egg. The anticipation is part of the ritual — no one knows which holds the rabbit within.

III
Discover

Break open together. Savour the chocolate. One person will find the hidden porcelain rabbit — a collectible charm, a symbol of fortune.

The hidden treasure

The Porcelain
Fractal Rabbit

Concealed within one egg lies a piece of mathematical art. Crafted in Limoges, France — the capital of porcelain — and glazed in the tradition of Ming Dynasty Blue & White ware, the rabbit charm is not a toy but a collector's object.

Its surface is sculpted using Voronoi tessellation — a mathematical pattern found in nature, from dragonfly wings to giraffe coats — creating a delicate lace-like structure in fine white porcelain. A pendant. A desk stone. A symbol of fortune.
